About Texas Web Design

Founded in 2015 by a team of passionate web developers and designers, Texas Web Design emerged from a simple vision: to provide Texas businesses with world-class websites at affordable prices. What started as a small studio in Austin has grown into the state's leading web design agency, serving over 500 satisfied clients across Texas.

Our mission is to empower Texas businesses with powerful online presences that drive growth and success. We believe in combining cutting-edge technology with timeless design principles, delivering websites that not only look stunning but also perform exceptionally. Our core values of transparency, innovation, and client satisfaction guide everything we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, free hosting, SSL certificate, and 12 months of maintenance and support. Every website is built with modern technologies and optimized for performance.

How long does it take to complete a website?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity and client feedback. We follow a structured timeline: 3-5 days for design approval, 7-10 days for development, and 3-5 days for testing and revisions.

What makes your websites SEO-friendly?

Our websites include semantic HTML, optimized meta tags, schema markup, responsive images, XML sitemaps, and integration with Google Analytics and Search Console. We also provide on-page SEO optimization for key pages.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes, all packages include 12 months of support and maintenance, covering security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and technical support. Extended support plans are available after the first year.

What is your pricing structure?

Our packages start at $999 for basic websites and scale based on functionality requirements. We offer flexible payment plans and transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Custom quotes are provided after initial consultation.
